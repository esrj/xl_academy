獲得 WASC 認證

WASC（Western Association of Schools and Colleges） 是美國西部學校與學院認證協會，負責評鑑學術機構的教學質量與認證標準。Afficient Academy 獲得 WASC 認證，代表我們的課程：

  • 符合美國學術標準，確保教學品質
  • 課程內容可銜接各大國際學校與標準考試
  • 提供學生穩固的學術基礎，為未來升學做好準備

WASC Accreditation

WASC (Western Association of Schools and Colleges) is an accreditation organization responsible for evaluating the academic quality and standards of educational institutions in the western United States. Afficient Academy has earned WASC accreditation, signifying that our programs:

  • Meet U.S. academic standards to ensure high-quality education
  • Align with international schools and standardized exams
  • Provide a strong academic foundation for future education
