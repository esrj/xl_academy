為什麼大學申請比以往更加嚴苛

  • 學校期待獨特的個人故事，而不僅僅是考試成績
  • 許多申請因為文章撰寫不佳或活動經歷過於平凡而失敗
  • 學生不了解如何打造自己的「亮點」或定位
  • 截止日期、申請平台（Common App、UCAS）及申請要求因國家而異
  • 若無指導，許多學生申請範圍過廣或缺乏策略

事實上，強而有力的申請不僅取決於「聰明」，更在於策略、時機與敘事。

Why College Admissions Are Tougher Than Ever

  • Schools expect unique personal stories, not just test scores
  • Many applications fail due to poor essay writing or generic activities
  • Students don’t understand how to build their "hook" or positioning
  • Deadlines, portals (Common App, UCAS), and requirements vary by country
  • Without guidance, many students apply too broadly or without strategy

The truth: A strong application is not just about being “smart” — it’s about strategy, timing, and storytelling.
