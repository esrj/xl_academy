AP課程輔導

為什麼AP課程對台灣學生具挑戰性

  • 學校課程進度迅速
  • 自學者支援有限
  • GPA與SAT壓力過大
  • 英文術語複雜，特別是AP物理、AP化學與AP微積分
  • 缺乏AP歷史與AP心理學的考試策略、時間分配及寫作練習
  • 即使是校內頂尖學生，若無外部協助，也常在AP考試中表現不佳

自學AP vs. 校內AP課程

類別 自學陷阱 我們提供
課程大綱 無結構化大綱或教師支援 系統化大綱與專業教師指導
實驗支援 無實驗室設備使用（AP 理科） 完整實驗室實作與指導
學分認證 無官方學校成績單學分 正式學校成績單記錄
進度節奏 校內課程進度過快，無充分複習 彈性進度安排與加強複習

AP報名與截止日期

  • 多數AP考試報名於11月初截止
  • 逾期報名費用更高且可能影響考場選擇
  • 學生須透過學校或 College Board 認可考點報名（我們可協助）

📣 若您的孩子採自學方式，須透過外部測驗中心報名 — 無支援下程序較為複雜。

為何家長選擇我們

  • 雙語支援：提供英語及中文授課
  • 經驗豐富師資：講師曾獲AP 5分或主修相關科目
  • 彈性排課：協助同時準備IB、SAT或校內考試的學生
  • 一對一與小班制：依學習習慣最佳化授課
  • 衝刺課程：考前1個月集中訓練（90%學員無基礎即可獲3分以上）

AP course tutoring

Why AP Classes Are Challenging for Students in Taiwan

  • Fast-paced curricula at school
  • Limited support for self-learners
  • High pressure from GPA and SAT demands
  • Complex English terminology, especially in AP Physics, AP Chemistry, and AP Calculus
  • Lack of exam strategy, timing, and writing practice for AP History and AP Psychology
  • Even top-performing students in local schools often underperform in AP exams without outside help

Self-Studying vs. Taking AP Classes in School

類別 自學陷阱 我們提供
課程大綱 No structured syllabus or teacher support Systematic syllabus and professional teacher support
實驗室使用 No lab access (for AP Sciences) Full lab practice and guided instruction
學分認證 No official school transcript credit (may hurt college apps) Official transcript credit recorded
進度節奏 Schools may rush through materials without time to absorb or review Flexible pacing with thorough review sessions

AP Registration & Deadlines

  • Most AP exam sign-ups close by early November
  • Late sign-ups cost more and may limit test center access
  • Students must be registered through their school or College Board-approved test centers (we can assist with this)

If your child is self-studying, they must register through an external testing center — and this can be complicated without support.

Why Parents Choose Us

  • Bilingual Support: Offering classes in both English or Mandarin
  • Experienced AP Instructors: Each instructor has scored a 5 on the exam or majored in the subject
  • Custom Schedules: Help students juggling IB, SAT, or local exams
  • 1-on-1 and Small Group Classes: Optimized to your learning habits; 1-on-1 or small groups for AP programs
  • Crash Courses: 1-month intensive courses before exam season (90% of participants score 3+ with no prior experience)

數位SAT課程輔導

為何數位SAT比以往更具挑戰性

  • 自適應題組（題目越難，壓力越大）
  • 更緊湊的數位考試節奏帶來時間壓力
  • 閱讀理解與文法的新題型
  • 數學部分計算器使用規則變更
  • 缺乏紙本模擬考或針對DSAT格式的實用策略
  • 即使具備良好英語與數學基礎，因不熟悉考試形式，成績經常偏低

自學DSAT適合嗎？

類別 自學陷阱 我們提供
資源 不符合DSAT考試風格的過時資源 DSAT專屬練習與模擬
管道 無法使用數位自適應模擬平臺 真實自適應模擬考試與進度追蹤
回饋 對寫作或閱讀錯誤的回饋有限 一對一輔導與詳細分析
節奏 缺乏節奏管理策略 具信心的完卷技巧

📣 大多數自學者每月僅進步20–30分，而我們的平均學員在2–3個月內進步80–150+分。

DSAT重要日期與資訊（2025）

  • DSAT考試場次：3月、5月、8月、10月、12月
  • 報名於考前約1個月截止
  • 大多數美國大學（尤其百大）要求DSAT成績
  • 成績選擇政策可只寄送最佳成績
  • 部分大學採免測政策，但CS、醫科、商科等競爭科系仍重視高分
  • 台灣考場特別是TAS與交大考場很快額滿，我們協助提前規劃

為何家長信任XL Academy的DSAT課程

  • 雙語輔導：英語或中文授課，舒適清晰
  • 客製學習計畫：依初診結果與校內課表安排
  • 高分師資：1500–1580分得主或常春藤/美國前十大校友
  • 一對一/小班制：專注、精準教學
  • 數位模擬考：真實自適應模擬考場體驗
  • 衝刺課程＆密集營：考前最後衝刺，適合時間有限的學生

Digital SAT course tutoring

Why the Digital SAT Is More Challenging Than Ever

  • Adaptive question modules (harder questions = higher stakes)
  • Time pressure with tighter digital format pacing
  • New question types in reading comprehension and grammar
  • Calculator usage changes in the math section
  • Lack of access to paper mock exams or proven strategies for the DSAT format
  • Even students with strong English and Math foundations often score lower on the DSAT due to unfamiliarity with the format

Should You Self-Study for the DSAT?

Category Self-Study Pitfalls What We Provide
Resources Outdated resources that don’t match DSAT style DSAT-specific drills & simulations
Access No access to digital adaptive mock platforms Real adaptive mock tests & progress tracking
Feedback Limited feedback on writing or reading errors 1-on-1 coaching with detailed analysis
Pricing Lack of pacing strategies Proven techniques to finish on time with confidence

📣 Most self-studiers improve only 20–30 points per month. Our average student improves 80–150+ points in 2–3 months.

Important Deadlines & Facts for DSAT (2025)

  • DSAT test dates: March, May, August, October, December
  • Registration closes ~1 month before test date
  • DSAT is required for most U.S. universities, especially top 100 schools
  • Score Choice policy allows students to send only their best score
  • Some universities are test-optional, but CS, Pre-Med, Business remain score-sensitive
  • Taiwan test centers fill quickly — we help plan ahead

Why Parents Trust XL Academy for DSAT

  • Bilingual Coaching: Classes in English or Mandarin for comfort and clarity
  • Customized Study Plans: Based on initial diagnostics & school schedule
  • Top-Scoring Instructors: 1500–1580 scorers or Ivy League/US Top 10 grads
  • 1-on-1 or Small Group Classes: Focused, targeted instruction
  • Digital Mock Exams: Fully adaptive to simulate real test-day experience
  • Crash Courses & Intensive Camps: Last-minute boosts for busy students
